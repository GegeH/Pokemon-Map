What is myPokemon map?

This Pokemon map shows you the real-time locations of pokemons and the estimated time they are going to disappear based on Pokemon Go App Game.

You can find all Pokemon near you in real time for Pokemon Go App Game. Pokemon will be marked on the map along with their disappearence count down timer on the map. The map marker is real time, meaning they are currently exists in the Pokemon Go app and can be found exact same spot on the map.

Why there is no pokemon in my area?

Open the map and zoom in to the area you can reach, wait for a little while. Then keep your web page open so that map will update automatically.

Please zoom in and keep wait. It might take a while to check different location and update the map. If you do not see pokemon more than 10 minutes, please contact support@mypokemon.io
